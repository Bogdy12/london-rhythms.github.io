London Night Rhythms
The night-time economy is fundamental for Londonâ€™s success as a global city. The visualisations aim to explore the pulse of the night-time economy at different scales. By exploring how these patterns interact and influence one another, we hope to understand it better as a whole.

  • How do people move around at night?

    Using TFL data to visualise exit and entry passenger flow.

  • What can people do?

    Using yelp data to explore patterns of nightlife venue hours across the city.

  • Where do people go at night?

    Using CDRC footfall data to visualise individual level movement patterns.

  • How active are people in different areas at night?

    Using twitter data to explore patterns of social vitality during the night.
