Rhythms of the Night

A visual exploration of the pulse of London's Night Time Economy

Aims

“cities – or parts thereof – are constituted by intricate mixtures of rhythms, and questions about the factors producing rhythmic conformity are central to understanding night-time economies and cities more generally”
(Schwanen et al., 2012)

Institutionally Inscribed Rhythms

This visualisation uses data gathered from the Yelp API to examine the patterns of the opening and closing hours of the businesses typically associated with the night time economy.
